About RCIS

At RCIS we believe that students are responsible for the future of the country and the world at large. The education of the child therefore decides the fate of the world. And this responsibility is vested in the schools, and we take this responsibility seriously.

Knowledge is power but here at RCIS we believe that knowledge alone is not enough, it is very essential to bring out the personality of the student, get the child ready to face the world of tomorrow.

our mission

RCIS aims for the development of children into well-disciplined and academically bright and self-confident individuals, who will move on to mark in the world and achieve their best in all spheres of life.

The friendly and congenial learning environment ensures all-round growth of each child in the physical, mental, social and spiritual spheres of life.
