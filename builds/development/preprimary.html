Pre-Primary Level

Helping the children express, through arts, crafts and colors, children are given creative freedom, helping them learn through carefully planned, activity based learning. Conceptual learning is encouraged through activities, field trips and outings. Emphasis is given on building confidence and self-expression through Rhyme time, Show and tell, Talent Shows and more.

Guiding children in bringing about their best social and emotional conduct through thoughtfully crafted activities involving toy parties, color of the month and celebration of major festivals, thereby giving children their space and at the same time helping them learn.

Assessments

The Pre-School program is based on continuous classroom work, theme based workseets and observations of the child. A report card will outline the various aspects of the child's development three times a year. The school follows a non-detention policy in the pre-primary classes.
