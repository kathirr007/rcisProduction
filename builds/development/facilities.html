Library cum reading room

RCIS has a modern and impressive Library of 2500 sq ft that is built to accommodate close to a hundred students at any given time. The library is well stocked with reading materials that interests different age groups. With an extensive collection of reference books, encyclopedias, dailies, magazines and journals the library is meant to provide valuable research material for class projects. The teachers and the librarian guide the students in using the library.

Laboratories

The school has a multi-purpose laboratory to meet the CBSE requirements that covers General Science which includes Physics, Chemistry and Biology up to secondary level. High quality specialised labs for Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Computer Science for the Plus II classes have been set up, as the labs form an integral part of their academics.

Computer Center

Two very well equipped Computer labs are set up for the students. Educomp Smart classes - a curriculum specific interactive classroom software has been successfully implemented resulting in better understanding. Smart Boards have changed the way children learn abstract concepts.

Auditorium

An auditorium with state-of-the-art infrastructure provides the perfect presentation platform, where the children display their talents on stage. This modern auditorium has all the latest facilities for sound and light effects, and has tiered seating to accommodate more than 400 people. It provides an exciting backdrop for performances in music, dance, theatre, debate and more.
