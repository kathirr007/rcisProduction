Primary Level

The primary curriculum is based on the NCERT frame work as per the CBSE syllabus. In addition to core subjects students will have holistic learning through value education, arts, crafts and sports activities.

Assessments

The students have continuous assessments through Unit Tests, Class Tests, Work sheets, Mental Maths and Dictations as part of the evaluation process, the assessment also include the conduct, discipline and the participation of the student in the activities of the school. Progress reports of the child will be discussed and handed over during the Parent - Teacher Meetings.
