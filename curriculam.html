Pre-Primary

Helping the children express, through arts, crafts and colors, children are given creative freedom, helping them learn through carefully planned, activity based learning. Conceptual learning is encouraged through activities, field trips and outings. Emphasis is given on building confidence and self-expression through Rhyme time, Show and tell, Talent Shows and more.

Primary

The primary curriculum is based on the NCERT frame work as per the CBSE syllabus. In addition to core subjects students will have holistic learning through value education, arts, crafts and sports activities.

Middle

The three language policy of CBSE is followed by the school. Hindi, Kannada & Sanskrit/French are offered as the 3rd languages at this stage.

The students are encouraged to learn through experimentation and practical activities, and the curriculum is based on team work. The Basic concepts of Math and Science are taught through activities in math and Science laboratories.

Secondary

The School follows an integrated two year curriculum and assessment by CBSE. This Scheme aims at holistic development of students. This strategy paves way for regular and continuous evaluation whereby keeping track of the academics and helping them improve consistently.

Senior-Scondary

At the senior secondary level the school will offer Science and Computer Science. RCIS plans to add Arts and Commerce streams in the near future. Well qualified faculty with decades of experience, train the students here. This will help them reach a higher potential which will pave the way for a more confident step towards graduation.
